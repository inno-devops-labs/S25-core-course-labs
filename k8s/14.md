#### Monitoring & Alerts
Added liveness and readiness probes to ensure pod health.

- Liveness Probe: Ensures the pod is running. If it fails, the pod is restarted.

- Readiness Probe: Ensures the pod is ready to serve traffic. If it fails, the pod is removed from service endpoints.

#### Ordering Guarantee and Parallel Operations
Set `podManagementPolicy: Parallel` to disable ordered deployment.

- StatefulSets ensure ordered deployment and scaling. For stateless apps, this is unnecessary.
